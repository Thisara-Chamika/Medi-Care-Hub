1. Diabetes

Causes: Diabetes is often caused by a combination of genetic factors and lifestyle choices. If a person has a family history of diabetes, they are more likely to develop it. Poor dietary habits—especially high sugar and processed food intake—and a lack of physical activity can increase the risk.

Symptoms: Common symptoms of diabetes include:

  • Increased thirst (polydipsia)
  • Frequent urination (polyuria)
  • Fatigue or tiredness even after rest
These symptoms occur due to elevated blood sugar levels, which affect the body’s ability to use glucose for energy properly.

Prevention: To reduce the risk of developing diabetes, individuals should:

  • Eat a balanced, healthy diet rich in vegetables, whole grains, and low in sugar
  • Engage in regular physical activity (e.g., walking, cycling, or gym workouts)
  • Maintain a healthy body weightthrough consistent habits

Treatment:Diabetes can be managed effectively through:

  • Medication such as Metformin (for Type 2)
  • Insulin therapy (essential for Type 1)
  • Making lifestyle changes like meal planning, exercise, and blood sugar monitoring
Early diagnosis and proper management can help prevent complications and lead to a healthy life.

2. Dengue

Causes: Mosquito bites (Aedes aegypti).

Symptoms: Fever, body aches, rash, bleeding.

Prevention: Use mosquito repellent, eliminate standing water.

Treatment: Rest, fluids, pain relievers (avoid aspirin).

3. COVID-19

Causes: Coronavirus (SARS-CoV-2) transmission.

Symptoms: Fever, cough, loss of taste/smell, breathing difficulty.

Prevention: Vaccination, hand washing, mask wearing.

Treatment: Rest, oxygen support, antiviral medication.

4. Influenza

Causes: Influenza virus (types A, B, C).

Symptoms: Fever, chills, cough, sore throat, muscle aches.

Prevention: Annual vaccination, hand hygiene.

Treatment: Antiviral drugs, rest, hydration.

5. Heart Disease

Causes: High blood pressure, high cholesterol, smoking.

Symptoms: Chest pain, shortness of breath, fatigue.

Prevention: Healthy diet, regular exercise, avoid smoking.

Treatment: Medication, lifestyle changes, surgery if necessary.

6. Cancer

Causes: Genetic mutations, environmental factors, lifestyle choices.

Symptoms: Varies by type; common signs include unexplained weight loss, fatigue, pain.

Prevention: Healthy lifestyle, regular screenings, avoiding known carcinogens.

Treatment: Surgery, chemotherapy, radiation therapy, immunotherapy.
