Prevention Tips

Explore key ways to prevent common diseases and lead a healthier life.

1. Healthy Lifestyle Habits

Engage in regular physical activity, avoid smoking and alcohol, and manage stress effectively. Maintain a balanced work-life routine to support overall well-being.

2. Hygiene Tips

Wash hands regularly with soap, maintain personal cleanliness, and sanitize frequently touched surfaces. Practicing good hygiene helps prevent the spread of infections and viruses.

3. Food & Nutrition Advice

Eat a diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins. Avoid excessive sugar, salt, and processed foods. Drink enough water and maintain regular meal times.

4. Vaccination & Early Detection

Stay up to date with recommended vaccines. Regular health check-ups, screenings, and early diagnosis improve treatment success and prevent disease progression.
